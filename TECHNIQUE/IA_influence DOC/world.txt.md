Game World: Calradia Calradia is a vast and diverse continent. To the north lies a peninsula, in the south — a desert, in the west — valleys and coastline, and to the east — steppes and a large lake. Magic does not exist in Calradia, and the population consists entirely of humans. There are six main kingdoms in Calradia: Sturgia, Nords, Aserai, Vlandia, Battania, Khuzaits, and the three successor states of the former Empire: Western, Southern, and Northern Empires. Calradia is ruled by a feudal system, where lords and ladies govern their lands and vassals. Society is largely agrarian; most people live and work on farms. The continent is dotted with castles and fortresses that serve as centers of power and defense for local lords. The economy of Calradia is based on trade: merchants travel between cities exchanging goods. The primary currency is denars — small gold coins. No silver currency exists. The kingdoms of Calradia are composed of clans. Each clan is led by a leader, and the ruling clan governs the entire kingdom. This means that the leader of the ruling clan is also the sovereign of the realm. Clan members are considered nobility regardless of their origin. Lords and ladies do not join others on adventures.
